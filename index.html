About us

Ever wanted to meet people and play fun games at the same time? Event Forge is the place to do so! We provide a space to create and join events for gaming themed events all around the Eindhoven area- Places to play both digitally and physically.

Create your own event

Have a board game you've always wanted to try? A campaign you have to tell the story of? Or maybe even a game queue you need filled up? Create an event to get people coming to your gaming event! Provide us just a bit of information and we'll be able to get your gaming event hosted to as many people as we can!

Rent a game room!

Small apartment and can't host a game of Root? Need a table for your campaign of waterdeep, or a screen for your smash tournament! Don't worry! We'll provide you with spaces to rent or even find a room so you can start fighting against whatever faction, dragon, or metaknight you need to beat!
